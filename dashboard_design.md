# Power BI Dashboard Plan

## Dashboard 1: Executive Overview
KPIs:
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value
- Retention Rate

Visuals:
- KPI Cards
- Monthly Revenue Line Chart
- Revenue by Category Bar Chart
- Orders Trend

## Dashboard 2: Customer 360
KPIs:
- Total Customers
- Repeat Customers
- Average Customer Lifetime Value

Visuals:
- Top 10 Customers by Revenue
- Customer Count by City
- Customer Age Distribution
- CLV Table

## Dashboard 3: Product Performance
KPIs:
- Total Products
- Top Product Category
- Best Selling Product

Visuals:
- Revenue by Product Category
- Top Products by Revenue
- Quantity Sold by Product

## Dashboard 4: Funnel Analysis
Funnel Stages:
- Page View
- Product View
- Add to Cart
- Checkout
- Purchase

Visuals:
- Funnel Chart
- Unique Customers by Event Type
- Conversion Drop-off

## Dashboard 5: Cohort & Retention
KPIs:
- Repeat Customer Rate
- Retention Rate
- Monthly Active Customers

Visuals:
- Cohort Table
- Retention Trend
- Repeat Customers vs New Customers

## Business Insights
This dashboard helps business teams understand revenue growth, customer behavior, product performance, and funnel conversion opportunities.
