# Project Metrics & KPIs

## Revenue KPIs

### Total Revenue
Formula:
Revenue = SUM(revenue)

### Monthly Revenue Growth
Formula:
(Current Month Revenue - Previous Month Revenue) / Previous Month Revenue

### Average Order Value (AOV)
Formula:
Total Revenue / Total Orders

---

## Customer KPIs

### Total Customers
COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id)

### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
SUM(revenue) by customer

### Repeat Customer Rate
Repeat Customers / Total Customers

---

## Product KPIs

### Top Product Revenue
SUM(revenue) by product

### Revenue by Category
SUM(revenue) by category

### Product Sales Volume
SUM(quantity)

---

## Funnel KPIs

### Page View Count
COUNT(Page View)

### Product View Count
COUNT(Product View)

### Add to Cart Count
COUNT(Add to Cart)

### Checkout Count
COUNT(Checkout)

### Purchase Count
COUNT(Purchase)

### Conversion Rate
Purchases / Page Views

---

## Retention KPIs

### Retention Rate
Returning Customers / Total Customers

### Monthly Active Customers
COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id)

### Cohort Retention
Customers retained by cohort month
