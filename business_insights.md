# Business Insights

## Revenue Insights
- Electronics generated highest revenue.
- Revenue increased year-over-year.

## Customer Insights
- Top 10% customers contributed significant revenue.
- Repeat customers generated higher CLV.

## Product Insights
- Electronics and Home categories performed best.
- Several products showed low sales volume.

## Funnel Insights
- Largest drop-off occurred between Add to Cart and Checkout.
- Purchase conversion can be improved.

## Retention Insights
- Returning customers have higher average order values.
- Customer retention improved over time.
